Glossary

Amplitude
Size of a wave’s oscillation; squared amplitude gives detection probability.
Eigenvalue
The specific, allowed value obtained when measuring a quantum property like energy or spin. An electron in an atom, for instance, can only have discrete energy levels, and these specific levels are its eigenvalues.
ħ
Reduced Planck constant, equal to h / (2π).
Observable
Measurable property of a system (e.g., energy, spin).
Wavefunction
Mathematical object encoding probabilities for outcomes.
