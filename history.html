Milestones

  1. 1900: Planck introduces the quantization of energy levels.
  2. 1905: Einstein proposes the photon theory of light.
  3. 1913: Bohr develops the model of the hydrogen atom.
  4. 1926: Schrödinger introduces wave mechanics and his equation.
  5. 1927: Heisenberg formulates the uncertainty principle.
  6. 1928: Dirac unifies quantum mechanics and special relativity.
  7. 1949: Feynman develops quantum electrodynamics.
